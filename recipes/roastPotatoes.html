Description

Roast potatoes with a chrispy skin. Easy to make, great on its own or as part of a meal.

Ingredients

  • New potatoes
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Crush the garlic and chop the thyme, add it to the olive oil, season with salt and pepper.
  2. Wash the potatoes, then cut in half. Toss in the olive oil mixture.
  3. Roast for 30 minutes at 200 degrees.
  4. Remove from the oven, turn the potatoes.
  5. Roast for another 25 - 30 minutes untill the skins are golden brown.
