Simple Pancakes

Description

Simple British pancakes, easy to make and go well with sugar and lemmon.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup all purpose flour (plain flour)
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 eggs (large)
  • 1/2 tablespoon butter (for cooking, approximation)

Steps

  1. Sift the flour into a bowl, add the milk and eggs. Wisk well.
  2. Melt butter into a pan on medium-high heat.
  3. Reduce the heat and coat the pan in the mixture.
  4. Let cook for a few minutes until the edges start to brown. Flip the pancake.
  5. Let cook for another minute-30 seconds until the other side has browned slightly.
  6. Put the pancake on a warm plate to the side, repeat steps 3-6 untill all the mixture is used.
