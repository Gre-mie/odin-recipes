Description

These roasted carrots can be used as a side or as part of a meal.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Crush the garlic, add to the olive oil with salt and pepper.
  2. Wash and peel the carrots. Chop them evenly.
  3. Roast for 15 minutes at 220 degrees.
  4. Take the charrots out of the oven, add the olive oil mixture and toss.
  5. Roast for another 15 minutes.
